About

What we noticed

  • There were 205 reported bike accidents from 2008 to 2010
  • All 2009 data was removed from mapping as it did not include any street information
  • The peak months for bike accidents were May through August; August consistently having the most
  • 62% of all bike accidents happened at street intersections
  • Accidents is trending downwards! In 2008 there were 87 reported, 2009 there were 61 and in 2010 there were only 57. From 2008 to 2010 that is a 34% decrease! Congrats Omaha!
  • You have a crazy amount of parks (162). Enjoy them!

Data Sources

Data was collected from a multiple sources

  • City of Omaha - Park Directory -- KML data
  • From YOU through our Park rating survey
  • HackOmaha -- Bike Accident Data
  • Data used to generate the BatchGeo map -- google docs
